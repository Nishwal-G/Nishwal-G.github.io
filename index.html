Welcome to my Homepage!

Welcome to my website. Alongside serving as a space for experimenting with HTML and JavaScript, it is primarily a record of the academic projects and research I have undertaken over the past five years. I have made an effort to design the site to be accessible, including for readers with colour-vision deficiencies and reading difficulties. If there are ways I can improve its inclusivity, please feel free to get in touch!
