What I Learned

During this project, I gained hands-on experience in analyzing the Zeeman effect in Kramers doublets. I learned how magnetic fields cause energy level splitting, applied quantum mechanics principles to predict spectral shifts, and practiced using numerical simulation tools to model the results. This project also helped me strengthen my understanding of spin-orbit coupling and its influence on spectral lines.
