Senior Honours Project – CoTiO₃

Download Project PDF

What I Learned

In this project, I studied the structural and magnetic properties of cobalt titanate (CoTiO₃) using experimental data and theoretical models. I developed a deeper understanding of crystal field theory and the effects of spin-orbit coupling on magnetic anisotropy. I also gained valuable experience in data analysis, fitting experimental results to theoretical curves, and presenting my findings in a clear and structured report. This project significantly improved my skills in scientific writing and technical problem-solving.
